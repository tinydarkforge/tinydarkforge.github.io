How we work

Principles that guide everything we build.

01

Local-first by default

Your data stays on your machine. No cloud dependency, no telemetry, no vendor lock-in. If it doesn't work offline, it's not done.

02

Ship what lasts

We build for maintainability over novelty. Clear scope, careful build, steady follow-through. No frameworks for the sake of frameworks.

03

Privacy is the default

Every product we ship is auditable, self-contained, and respects the user's autonomy. If we can't see your data, we can't leak it.
